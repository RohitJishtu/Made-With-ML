# 10-Week Schedule

~10–15 hours per week. MLOps-only cohorts stop after Week 8.

| Week | Focus | Hours |
|------|-------|-------|
| 1 | Foundations & system design | 10 |
| 2 | Data engineering | 12 |
| 3 | Modeling & training | 15 |
| 4 | Evaluation & tuning | 12 |
| 5 | Serving & APIs | 12 |
| 6 | Testing & CI/CD | 12 |
| 7 | Docker & Kubernetes | 15 |
| 8 | Monitoring & MLOps capstone | 15 |
| 9 | AIOps: RAG & LLM serving | 12 |
| 10 | AIOps: Eval & guardrails | 15 |

## Phases

1. **Build (1–4)** — data → train → evaluate → track
2. **Ship (5–6)** — serve API → automate with CI/CD
3. **Operate (7–8)** — containerize → monitor → capstone
4. **AI ops (9–10)** — RAG → eval → guardrails → capstone

## Suggested weekly rhythm

| Day | Activity |
|-----|----------|
| Mon | Read the week guide |
| Tue–Thu | Hands-on labs in your own repo |
| Fri | Complete deliverable + notes |

## Pacing options

| Pace | Approach |
|------|----------|
| Full-time | 20 h/week — finish in 5–6 weeks |
| Standard | 10–15 h/week — 10 weeks |
| Part-time | 5 h/week — one module every two weeks |
| MLOps only | Weeks 1–8, return later for 9–10 |
